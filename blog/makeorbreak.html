Breakfast can Make or Break your day.

What’s the first thing that comes to mind when you wake up in the morning? Probably how much you want to go back to sleep. Regardless, your breakfast, too, is undoubtedly one of the things that enter your mind every morning. Breakfast is a crucial part of your diet because it breaks the overnight fasting period, replenishes your supply of glucose and provides other essential nutrients to keep your energy levels up throughout the day. Despite all that, many adults are skip breakfast - maybe because they’re late or just don’t want to bother preparing anything so early in the morning.


Here are some benefits of a healthy breakfast.

Stay focused

A fulfilled tummy helps you feel more focused throughout the day, whether you’re at work, at school or just staying home. Eating food in the morning not only fuels us until lunchtime but actually gives our brains the essential energy to function and focus better on tasks throughout the day.

Controls your appetite

When you eat breakfast you're telling your body that there are plenty of calories to be had for the day. When you skip breakfast the message your body gets is that it needs to conserve rather than burn any incoming calories.

Energise yourself

Without breakfast you are effectively running on empty, like trying to start the bike with no petrol! As soon as we start eating in the morning, we kickstart our body chemistry which gets us feeling energized and ready for the day.
