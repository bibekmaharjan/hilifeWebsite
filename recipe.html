Pancetta, Fried Egg, and Red-Eye Gravy Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 1/2 ounce pancetta, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons brewed black coffee
  • 2 tablespoons tomato juice
  • 1 large egg Dash of black pepper
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
      • Step 1

      Heat a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat; add pancetta to pan.

      Step 2

      Bring water to a boil in a medium saucepan. Stir in oats and dash of salt. Reduce heat;
