Did You Know?

Oats are loaded with dietary fiber (containing more than many other grains) and have a range of healthy cholesterol-lowering properties. The researchers state that eating oats appears to help reduce hunger and increase feelings of fullness. Benefits include lower blood sugar and cholesterol levels, protection against skin irritation and reduced constipation. At the end of the day, oats are among the healthiest foods you can eat.
