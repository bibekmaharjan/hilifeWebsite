OUR STORY

OUR PRODUCTS

Satya Sai Group is a consortium of conglomerate Companies registered as per the Company Act of the Nepal Government with a Registration No. . It has, within its consortium fold, 8 independently associated Production and trade units namely – Nepal Everest Food & Snacks Pvt. Ltd. (2002), Everest Thread Industries (1997), Shree Mahalaxmi Match Industries Pvt. Ltd. (2006), Shree Mahalaxmi Trading Concern, Rising Sun Agro Industries Pvt. Ltd. (2016), Shiva Shakti Print & Pack Industries Pvt. Ltd. (2017), Hilife Foods Pvt. Ltd. (2019) and Satya Sai Exim Pvt. Ltd.

Nepal Everest Food & Snacks Pvt. Ltd. (NEFS)

NEFS was established in the year 2002 with a modest beginning with gram flour and cornflakes production which still stands as its root business. This must be considered a pioneering venture in Nepal as NEFS was the first Nepali food industry to begin the production and trade of these food items. Within few years of its marketing, the company was awarded the national recognition as the best quality food product produced in Nepal. Among the Nepali brands of similar products, the NEFS brands were liked by more buyers thus occupying over 70 percent of the market share. As a result, the number of employees in NEFS has grown from a modest beginning to over 100 employees. The products are sold all over Nepal with an estimated annual turnover of Rs.175 million.

Everest Thread Industries (ETI)

ETI was established in Nepal towards the end of the nineteenth century (1997). The core business was the manufacturing of the threads/yarns for sewing the garments. In the beginning, only one brand of yarn was produced, By now, there has been an exemplary growth in the types of yarns produced. Of these, 7 brands have reached a pinnacle of success in terms of largest selling brands. The yarns produced by ETI is now considered one of the best quality sewing thread in the region. The Industry is witnessing the current annual turn-over of over Rs.90 million.

Shree Mahalaxmi Match Industries Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 2006, the industry started to produce match sticks on a modest level. Now, the industry is proud to have been producing the highest number of match boxes in Nepal. The brand expansion has also been noteworthy, from a single brand to 5 brands now. The industry has now been employee numbering nearly 100 workers. The products are sold all over Nepal annually grossing Rs. 225 million.

Shree Mahalaxmi Trading Concern (SMTC)

This is a trade organization involved in importing the products from foreign destinations and selling those in Nepal. This business entity has now been importing and distributing over 50 products through its network all over the country. This large scale of business operations has been made possible through 7 well established network offices throughout the country. SMTC has now a share of nearly 80 percent of retail sales coverage in the whole country. With a sheer 50 employees, its annual turn-over has reached close to Rs. 400 million.

Rising Sun Agro Industries Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 2016, this facility has been producing merchandises such as beaten or flat rice, soya nuggets, puffed rice, cattle feed with 5 largest selling brand of products. This is the sole or exclusive beaten rice industry producing the product with state-of-the-art technology. The number of employees in the industry is more than 200 and the annual turn-over is around Rs.350 million.

Shiva Shakti Print & Pack Industries Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 2017, this industry chain is involved in the manufacturing of corrugated paper packaging boxes, and this scale of operation is the largest in the country. This plant is also the first fully automated facility in the region. With a sheer 50 employees, the industry is grossing Rs,600 million annually.

Hilife Foods Pvt. Ltd

Established in 2019, Hilife is involved in the manufacture of Oatmeals, Muesli, Choco Flakes, Soya Chunks, Cornflakes as its core business. In food products, Hilife has earned its repute as the most known brand in extremely short period of its first launch owing mainly to its quality and promotional campaigns. This has been the first Nepali brand of products with such diverse product ranges. The business coverage has been the whole country. With 50 dedicated staffs, the industry is faring well with nearly Rs. 600 million gross annual turn-over and growing.

Satya Sai Exim Pvt. Ltd.

This private trade industry is involved in the import and distribution of FMCG. It has been operating its distribution network with 5 well established offices in different locations all over Nepal. In the FMCG gross trade portfolio, it has over 80% of retail coverage in Nepal. The unique feature of its trade has been the “in-house branding team”. With over 50 employees, the annual turn-over is nearly 600 million.

OUR OBJECTIVES

The objectives of this Consortium has been;

  • To provide safe and hygienic food and snacks to the consumers at an affordable prices;
  • To provide quality articles and utility products used in religious ceremonies such as match boxes, holy threads;
  • To provide processed healthy agro-food items;
  • provide printing and packaging materials and services;
  • To boost employments of the unemployed youths.
